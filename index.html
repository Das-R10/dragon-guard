Capabilities

Built for environments
where security isn't optional

From open-plan offices to SCIF facilities — DragonGuard adapts to your threat model without adding operational friction.

01 — Detection
Proximity Detection

Multi-sensor fusion — Bluetooth, Wi-Fi RSSI, and motion — triangulates your presence with sub-metre accuracy.

02 — Response
Instant Lock

Screen locks the moment you step beyond the configured perimeter. No grace period by default — fully configurable per policy.

03 — Compliance
Tamper-Evident Audit Trail

Every lock, unlock, and anomaly logged with cryptographically signed timestamps. One-click export to SIEM.

04 — Architecture
Zero Cloud Dependency

Runs entirely on-device. Air-gapped and SCIF environments fully supported. Your proximity data never leaves the machine.

05 — Policy
Granular Policy Engine

Define rules by role, time-of-day, or physical zone. Enforce different lockout thresholds for executives vs. contractors.

06 — Platform
Cross-Platform Agent

macOS, Windows, and Linux. Single lightweight agent, unified management console, consistent behaviour across your fleet.

How It Works

Up and running
in under ten minutes

1
Install the agent

Single-package deployment. MDM-compatible via Jamf, Intune, or Puppet. Under two minutes per machine at scale.

2
Pair your presence anchor

DragonGuard learns your phone or access badge as the trusted anchor. No extra hardware or beacons required.

3
Walk away. It locks.

The moment signal loss is detected beyond your configured radius, the screen locks. Return — it unlocks. Zero friction.

4
Review the audit log

Every event captured, signed, and timestamped. Pull your compliance report in seconds, not hours.
